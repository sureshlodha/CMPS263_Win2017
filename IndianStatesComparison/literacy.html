Comparison of Indian States - Census 2011



India takes a census every 10 years. The last one was taken in 2011. This project visualizes three attributes of the census, namely the 'Population', 'Literacy' and 'Employment'.

In terms of Population, Uttar Pradesh comes first with 200 million and Sikkim comes last at 0.6 million

Kerela has the highest literacy rate at 94 % whereas Bihar ranks the lowest at 61.8%

As of percent employed out of total population, Himachal Pradesh has highest percentage 51.85% whereas Uttar Pradesh ranks the lowest at 32.9%

Last, but the not the least, murders per hundered thousand people is highest for Meghalaya (5.73) and lowest for Kerela (1.09)
